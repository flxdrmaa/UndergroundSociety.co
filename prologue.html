Prologue

Invincible Midnight Racer is a car club originating from the villages called Palomino. It started with three friends named Guerro Geronimo, Bastiano Delucca and Jose William who had a hobby of automotive. Guerro, Bestiano and Jose know a lot about the world of illegal racing and also modifying car components. The three of them also run a workshop business and sell illegal spare parts.

Their love for the automotive world and illegal activities such as illegal racing, modification of parts and engines that are prohibited by the government, made these three friends decide to establish a club called Invincible Midnight Racer.

Guerro "Gerry" Geronimo

Guerro "Gerry" Geronimo is the President of the Invincible Midnight Racer club who is in control of all activities in this club. Guerro is an expert in overhauling, replacing and improving engine performance, and doing drag races is his specialty.

Bastiano "Smokey" Delucca

Bastiano "Smokey" Delucca is the Vice President of the Invincible Midnight Racer club who helped Guerro Gerry Geronimo in developing this car club. Bastiano is an expert at drifting, winding tracks are not a problem for him.

Jose "Kaze" William

Jose "Kaze" William is the Treasurer of the Invincible Midnight Racer club who helps Guerro Gerry Geronimo and Bastiano Lucy Delucca in managing this club. Jose is an expert in long tracks that have 1 lap, or what is called a Sprint. His expertise is supported by a car made by Guerro.
